隐私政策
信息收集

Clippin 不收集任何个人数据。我们高度重视您的隐私，应用运行期间不会采集、传输或存储任何个人信息。

所有剪贴板内容仅保存在您的设备本地，应用不会访问您的通讯录、位置信息、相机、麦克风或其他任何系统服务。

数据存储

Clippin 使用 SQLite 本地数据库 存储您的剪贴板数据。所有数据均保存在您的设备上，不会通过网络传输。

为保持应用高效运行，应用启动时会自动清理 超过 7 天的非置顶 剪贴板内容。已置顶的内容将被永久保留，直到您手动删除。

权限说明

Clippin 仅使用以下系统权限：

剪贴板读取权限 — 用于监听和读取系统剪贴板内容，以便您能够管理和快速访问复制过的文本。这是 Clippin 的核心功能所需权限。

除此之外，Clippin 不请求、不使用任何其他系统权限。

第三方服务

Clippin 不使用任何第三方服务。应用不集成任何数据分析工具、广告 SDK、社交登录插件或任何其他第三方组件。

您的数据完全由您自己掌控，不存在任何数据共享或数据交换行为。

数据安全

Clippin 的所有数据均 存储在您的本地设备上，不会上传到任何服务器。应用不包含任何网络通信功能，因此不存在数据泄露的风险。

您的剪贴板数据完全在您的控制之下，卸载应用后所有数据将被自动清除。

儿童隐私

Clippin 不针对 13 岁以下儿童进行设计或营销。由于本应用不收集任何个人数据，因此不存在儿童个人信息保护方面的特殊风险。

如果您是家长或监护人，对儿童使用本应用有任何疑问，欢迎通过下方联系方式与我们沟通。

Information Collection

Clippin does not collect any personal data. We take your privacy seriously, and the app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information during operation.

All clipboard content is stored solely on your local device. The app does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other system services.

Data Storage

Clippin uses a SQLite local database to store your clipboard data. All data is kept on your device and is never transmitted over the network.

To maintain optimal app performance, the app will automatically clean up non-pinned clipboard content older than 7 days each time it starts. Pinned content is permanently retained until you manually delete it.

Permissions

Clippin requires only the following system permission:

Clipboard Read Access — Used to monitor and read system clipboard content so you can manage and quickly access copied text. This is essential for Clippin's core functionality.

Beyond this, Clippin does not request or use any other system permissions.

Third-Party Services

Clippin does not use any third-party services. The app does not integrate any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, social login plugins, or any other third-party components.

Your data is entirely under your own control, and there is no data sharing or data exchange of any kind.

Data Security

All Clippin data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server. The app does not include any network communication functionality, eliminating any risk of data breaches.

Your clipboard data is fully under your control. All data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Clippin is not designed or marketed for children under 13 years of age. Since this app does not collect any personal data, there are no special risks regarding children's personal information protection.

If you are a parent or guardian with questions about children's use of this app, please feel free to contact us using the information below.
